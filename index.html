Abstract: The Art of Design is a Netflix original documentary series highlighting artists in the field of design created by former Wired editor-in-chief Scott Dadich.
It was beautifully shot, showed a deep understanding of the various disciplines it explored, and most importantly, it featured great subjects. It takes you beyond blueprints into the art, science, and philosophy of design. From how we see the world to how we impact the world, the series goes inside the minds of the world‚Äôs greatest designers, showcasing the most inspiring visionaries from a variety of disciplines whose work shapes our culture and future. It was released on Netflix on February 10, 2017. In 2019, Netflix announced that the series had been renewed for a second season, which was released on September 25, 2019.
The first season featured illustrator Christoph Niemann, Nike shoe designer Tinker Hatfield, stage designer Es Devlin, architect Bjarke Ingels, automotive designer Ralph Gilles, graphic designer Paula Scher, photographer Platon and interior designer Ilse Crawford.
The second season profiled artist and architect Olafur Eliasson, bio architect Neri Oxman, costume designer Ruth Carter, toy designer Cas Holman, digital product designer Ian Spalter and typeface designer Jonathan Hoefler.
